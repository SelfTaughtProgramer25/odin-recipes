Mug Cake: flour, baking powder, chocolate, vaniella essence, oil

Home
  • ¼ cup all-purpose flour
  • ¼ cup white sugar
  • 2 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder
  • ⅛ teaspoon baking soda
  • ⅛ teaspoon salt
  • 3 tablespoons milk
  • 2 tablespoons canola oil
  • 1 tablespoon water
  • ¼ teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Gather all ingredients.
  • In a microwave-safe mug, mix the flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking soda, and salt.
  • Add the milk, canola oil, water, and vanilla extract to the dry ingredients.
  • Stir until well combined.
  • Microwave on high for about 1 minute and 30 seconds, or until the cake has risen and is set in the middle.
  • Let it cool for a minute before enjoying your mug cake.
